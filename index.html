About BYP-NOVA

We are a multidisciplinary university group from the University of Palermo and the University of Burgundy, united by a shared vision and supported by the FORTHEM Alliance. Together, we have launched the BYP-NOVA Project—a pioneering initiative focused on addressing the global challenge of food waste. Our goal is to transform food waste into valuable resources by combining scientific research, innovation, and sustainability. Through this collaboration, we aim to create a comprehensive knowledge hub that gathers and shares cutting-edge research, encourages sustainable practices, and supports new approaches to food waste valorisation.

Our vision: Circular economy for food

In a world grappling with resource scarcity and environmental concerns, the BYP-NOVA project envisions a future where food by-products are no longer considered waste but rather untapped goldmines. Our focus is on developing and promoting innovative scientific solutions to extract high-value compounds from these by-products, integrating them back into the economic cycle.

We believe in the power of interdisciplinary collaboration to drive meaningful change. By connecting researchers, industry professionals, policymakers, and the public, we facilitate the exchange of knowledge and accelerate the adoption of sustainable practices across the food sector.

What We Do

  • Cutting-Edge Research: We support and highlight research into novel processes for extracting bioactive compounds, proteins, fibers, and other valuable components from various food processing residues.
  • Knowledge Dissemination: We serve as a central platform for sharing scientific publications, best practices, case studies, and practical guidelines to empower stakeholders with the information they need.
  • Community Building: We foster a vibrant community of experts and enthusiasts committed to reducing food waste and building a more sustainable future.

From Waste Stream to Value Chain

The BYP-NOVA project is dedicated to the promotion and transformation of food by-products into high-value resources. We explore innovative pathways to create next-generation functional foods, advanced nutraceuticals, and sustainable packaging materials, building a truly circular economy for the food industry.

Our Core Objectives

Driving innovation through focused, collaborative action.

Share Innovative Research

Disseminate cutting-edge research findings, case studies, and success stories from the forefront of valorisation science.

Promote Circular Principles

Champion the principles of a circular economy by providing accessible knowledge and fostering a community of practice.

Highlight New Products

Showcase tangible examples of how food by-products are being successfully repurposed into valuable new materials and goods.
